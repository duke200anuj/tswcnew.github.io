Frequently Asked Questions

In which cities do we operate?

Currently we are providing our serivces in lucknow and kanpur,We provide free pickup delivery(specific range) in both cities.

Where can i drop/pick my shoe,if i want to?

You can drop or pick your shoe at our listed stores in both cities. Details coming soon!

How can i book my order ? & what is expected delivery time?

To schedule a pickup simply click on book now button and you will be redirected to our whatsapp support team,they will assist you from there. Usual delivery time for lucknow is 4 days & for kanpur is 3 days after pickup.

What will be given at time of pickup as proof of pickup and when will i have to pay for serivce?

You will be given a physical receipt and a token card with all details of your order and a final bill on whatsapp on delivery.You will pay for your service after checking your shoe when delivered.

What if my pickup or delivery is delayed or missed due to any reason?

Our pickup delivery team confirms atleast 4 hrs before reaching to your destination for pickup or delivery.If customer denies/is unavailable after giving confirmation on call,then customer has to wait for next pickup or delivery day route or has to pay 99/- extra as special pickup/delivery charge to avail the service.

What if my shoe gets damage again after getting fixed by you?

We request customer to check their article infront of delivery executives at time of delivery,if any issue is unresolved then we take it back to our workshop without any questions asked. We provide free complaints resolution within 3 days after delivery. For color complaints we entertain for 2 months.
